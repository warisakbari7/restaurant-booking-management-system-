# 1. Introduction

## 1.1 Purpose

The Restaurant Booking Management System (RBMS) is a portfolio project developed to demonstrate the complete software development lifecycle for a modern web application. The project follows a structured approach beginning with business analysis and continuing through requirements engineering, system design, implementation, testing, and deployment.

This Business Requirements Document (BRD) defines the business needs, project scope, stakeholder expectations, and high-level business requirements that guide the development of the system.

---

## 1.2 Business Need

Many restaurants still rely on phone calls, notebooks, or spreadsheets to manage reservations. These methods can lead to booking conflicts, inefficient table allocation, and unnecessary administrative work.

A centralized web application enables customers to make reservations online while allowing restaurant staff to manage bookings, tables, and restaurant operations more efficiently.

---

## 1.3 Project Goals

The primary goals of the Restaurant Booking Management System are to:

* Digitalize the restaurant reservation process.
* Improve table utilization.
* Reduce manual administrative work.
* Prevent double bookings.
* Improve the customer booking experience.
* Provide administrators with operational insights through reporting.
