# 2. Business Analysis

## 2.1 Business Overview

Bella Vista Restaurant is a fictional medium-sized restaurant created for this portfolio project to simulate a realistic business environment. The restaurant serves lunch and dinner seven days a week and accepts both walk-in customers and advance table reservations.

The restaurant currently manages reservations manually through telephone calls and handwritten reservation books. As customer demand increases, this approach becomes less efficient and more prone to errors.

The Restaurant Booking Management System (RBMS) is proposed to replace the manual reservation process with a centralized web application that simplifies reservation management and improves operational efficiency.

---

## 2.2 Current Business Process

The current reservation workflow consists of the following steps:

1. A customer contacts the restaurant by phone.
2. A staff member checks table availability manually.
3. The reservation is recorded in a notebook.
4. The customer receives verbal confirmation.
5. On the reservation date, staff locate the booking and assign a table.

Although this process is simple, it depends entirely on manual coordination and staff experience.

---

## 2.3 Business Problems

The current process introduces several operational challenges.

### Reservation Management

* Reservations are stored manually.
* Reservation updates require manual changes.
* Booking conflicts may occur during busy periods.
* Reservation history is difficult to retrieve.

### Table Management

* Table availability cannot be viewed in real time.
* Tables may remain unused because of inefficient allocation.
* Double-booking may occur.

### Customer Experience

* Customers cannot book online.
* Reservation confirmation depends on staff availability.
* Customers cannot view or manage their reservations.

### Administration

* Managers cannot monitor reservation statistics easily.
* Operational reports must be prepared manually.
* Customer information is not centrally managed.

---

## 2.4 Proposed Business Solution

The Restaurant Booking Management System will provide a centralized platform for managing reservations and restaurant operations.

The solution will allow customers to:

* Register and log in.
* Search available reservation times.
* Book a table online.
* Modify reservations.
* Cancel reservations.
* View reservation history.

Restaurant administrators will be able to:

* Manage reservations.
* Manage restaurant tables.
* Configure opening hours.
* Manage customer information.
* View operational reports.
* Monitor daily restaurant activities.

---

## 2.5 Expected Business Benefits

The proposed solution is expected to provide the following benefits:

### Operational Benefits

* Faster reservation processing.
* Reduced administrative workload.
* Improved table utilization.
* Better reservation accuracy.

### Customer Benefits

* Convenient online booking.
* Faster confirmation.
* Improved reservation management.
* Better customer experience.

### Business Benefits

* Centralized reservation data.
* Improved reporting.
* Better operational visibility.
* A scalable platform for future enhancements.
