# 8. Success Criteria and Future Enhancements

## 8.1 Introduction

This chapter defines how the success of Version 1.0 of the Restaurant Booking Management System (RBMS) will be measured and identifies potential enhancements for future releases.

---

## 8.2 Success Criteria

The project will be considered successful if it meets the following objectives:

* Customers can register and securely log in.
* Customers can create, modify, and cancel reservations.
* The system prevents double booking of tables.
* Staff can efficiently manage reservations.
* Administrators can manage restaurant settings and user accounts.
* Reservation and table information is stored accurately.
* User authentication and authorization work correctly.
* The application is responsive and usable on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.
* The system provides reliable performance during normal restaurant operations.

---

## 8.3 Future Enhancements

The following features are intentionally excluded from Version 1.0 but may be added in future releases:

* Online payment integration
* Email notifications
* SMS reminders
* Multi-branch restaurant support
* QR code check-in
* Loyalty and rewards program
* Waitlist management
* Customer reviews and ratings
* Online food ordering
* Mobile applications (Android and iOS)
* Integration with third-party reservation platforms
* Business analytics dashboard with advanced reporting

---

## 8.4 Conclusion

The Restaurant Booking Management System Version 1.0 focuses on delivering a reliable, secure, and maintainable reservation management solution. The defined success criteria ensure that the project satisfies its primary business objectives, while the planned future enhancements provide a clear direction for the system's continued evolution.
